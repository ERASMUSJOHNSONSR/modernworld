Key Navigation Methods:

  • **Links:** Click on highlighted text or images to jump to new pages.
  • **Back/Forward Buttons:** Use these in your browser to revisit previous pages or move forward to pages you've already seen.
  • **Address Bar (URL Bar):** Type a website's address (URL) directly into this bar and press Enter to go to that specific page.

Using Search Engines

Search engines are powerful tools that help you find information on the vast internet. Just type what you're looking for, and they'll show you relevant results.

Results will appear here as you type.

How Search Engines Work:

  • **Query:** You type keywords or a question into the search bar.
  • **Indexing:** The search engine has already "indexed" billions of web pages, creating a massive database.
  • **Results:** It quickly finds pages in its index that match your query and presents them as a list of links.
  • **Click & Explore:** You click on the most relevant link to visit the website.

Bookmarks: Saving Your Favorites

Bookmarks (or Favorites) allow you to save the addresses of websites you visit frequently, so you can quickly return to them without typing the URL every time.

How to Use Bookmarks:

  • **Adding:** Look for a star icon (⭐) or a "Bookmark" option in your browser's toolbar or menu. Click it to save the current page.
  • **Accessing:** Bookmarks are usually found in a "Bookmarks" menu, toolbar, or sidebar within your browser. Click a saved bookmark to instantly go to that page.
  • **Organization:** You can often create folders within your bookmarks to keep them organized.
