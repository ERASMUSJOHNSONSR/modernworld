Storage Showdown

Which storage is right for the job? Compare the key characteristics of Hard Drives and Solid State Drives using the interactive chart below.

Performance Radar

HDD (Hard Disk Drive)

The traditional workhorse. Offers vast amounts of storage for a low cost, perfect for large files and backups.

SSD (Solid State Drive)

The speed demon. With no moving parts, it provides lightning-fast access for your OS and apps.

The Takeaway

Choose an SSD for your operating system and primary applications to get the best speed and responsiveness. Use a larger HDD for mass storage of photos, videos, and documents where speed is less critical.
