Pro Tips for Effective Searching

Refine your search queries to find exactly what you need, even faster!

  • Use Keywords: Be specific with your search terms. Instead of "picture," try "vacation photo 2023."
  • Include File Extensions: If you know the file type, add its extension (e.g., "report.pdf" or "budget.xlsx").
  • Search by Content: Modern search functions can often look inside documents for keywords, not just in their names.
  • Filter Results: Most OS search interfaces allow you to filter by type (documents, images, videos), date modified, or size. Look for options like "Kind," "Date," or "Size."
  • Wildcards (Advanced): Use `*` to represent any characters (e.g., `report*` finds "report", "reports", "reporting").
