Course Overview

Course Goal

To equip beginners with the fundamental knowledge and practical skills required to confidently use a computer, navigate the internet, manage files, and utilize basic software applications safely and effectively.

Target Audience

Individuals who are new to computers, seniors, or anyone looking to refresh their basic computer literacy.
