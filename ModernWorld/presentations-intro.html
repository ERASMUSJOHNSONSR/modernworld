What is a "Slide"?

In presentation software, a "slide" is a single page or screen that contains text, images, charts, or other multimedia elements. A presentation is a sequence of these slides.

This is a Presentation Slide

Each slide is like a canvas where you arrange your content to tell a story or convey key information to your audience.

Purpose of Slides:

  • **Visual Aid:** Support your spoken message with visuals.
  • **Key Points:** Highlight important information concisely.
  • **Engagement:** Keep the audience interested with dynamic content.
  • **Structure:** Organize your presentation into logical sections.
