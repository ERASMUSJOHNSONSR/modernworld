Cell

A cell is the smallest unit in a spreadsheet, where you enter individual pieces of data. Each cell has a unique address (e.g., A1, B3).

Row

A row is a horizontal series of cells, identified by a number (e.g., Row 1, Row 2). Rows typically contain related records or entries.

Column

A column is a vertical series of cells, identified by a letter (e.g., Column A, Column B). Columns often represent different categories or types of data.
